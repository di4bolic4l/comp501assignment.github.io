Research Choices exmaples

Choice 1: Quantitative Research

Conduct a quantitative study using surveys to gather statistical data on smartphone usage and mental health.

Choice 2: Qualitative Research

Opt for qualitative research by conducting in-depth interviews to gain a deeper understanding of adolescents' experiences.

Choice 3: Mixed-Methods Approach

Combine quantitative and qualitative methods to triangulate findings and provide a comprehensive view of the topic.

Choice 4: Cross-Cultural Study

Explore cultural variations by conducting the study in different regions to assess how smartphone use impacts mental health globally.
