Risks and Challenges examples

Risk 1: Data Privacy Concerns

There is a risk of breaching data privacy laws when collecting and storing adolescents' sensitive information.

Risk 2: Limited Participation

Some adolescents may not want to participate in the study, leading to a potential lack of diversity in the sample.

Risk 3: Time and Resource Constraints

Conducting a comprehensive study requires significant time and resources, which may be limited.

Risk 4: Ethical Considerations

Ensuring ethical treatment of participants, especially minors, is a critical concern throughout the study.
